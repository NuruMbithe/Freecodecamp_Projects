Here's a time line of Wangari Maathai's Life:

  • 1940 - Wangari Muta Maathai is Born Central Highlands Kenya
  • 1960 - Wangari wins a Kennedy scholarship and begins university education in the United States (through 1966).
  • 1966 - Wangari Maathai becomes research assistant at University of Nairobi
  • 1969 - Wangari Muta Maathai marries Mwangi Mathai.
  • 1971 - becomes first woman to receive a PhD in East & Central Africa.
  • 1977 - Green Belt Movement founded by Wangari Maathai.
  • 1992 - Civic and Environmental Education workshops by the Green Belt Movement begin in response to tribal clashes prior to elections.
  • 1998 - Karura Forest privatization protested, Green Belt Movement starts planting only indigenous trees.
  • 2002 - Wangari Maathai elected to Parliament with 98% of the vote.
  • 2004 - Wangari Maathai awarded the Nobel Peace Prize.
  • 2011 -Wangari Maathai passed away on september 25,2011
